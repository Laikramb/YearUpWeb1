Jalen Tabor

Florida Gators Jalen Tabor is the number one ranked pure cornerback for 2017. LaQuon Treadwell, who was one of the top Wide Recievers in the 2016 Draft, claimed that the best DB on Florida was the "younger one", not 2016 1st round pick Vernon Hargreaves III. Tabor is a big and strong techician at 6 ft 1 in and 195 lbs. He will be looking to improve on what was a good year.
(40 tackles, 14 pass deflections, and 4 int)
Player Comparison: Vontae Davis Indianapolis Colts

Peppers has played snaps at both CB and Saftey last season. Head Coah Jim Harbaugh has also stated that Peppers will see snaps at linebacker. Peppers also played offensive which limited his stats.

Deshaun Watson

Clemson Tigers Deshaun Watson is the type of QB that exemplifies the future of the NFL. Clemson is one of the CFL powerhouses and Deshaun Watson has been a huge part of that. Watson finished 3rd place in Heisman votes and has led his team to multiple bowl victories. The Tigers may have lost to Alambama in the National Title Game, but that shouldn't take away all the great things that Watson has brought to the program. He is projected to be a player of the year candidate and finish high in the Heisman Rankings.
(4104 Yards, 35 TDs, and 13 INTS)
Player Comparison: Michael Vick

Brad Kaaya

Miami Hurricans Brad Kaaya is severely underrated. Thu Hurricans have been a bad team the past couple of years thanks to former Head Coach Al Golden. Now that he is gone, Mark Richt, the former Georgia Bulldog coach was hired. Richt should be able to help put the offense in the best position to sucseed. Kaaya should have an amazing year and take his team to a bowl game this year. Kaaya is a big, strong armed QB who has been a starter at Miami since his Freshman. Kaaya keeps getting better everytime I see him.
(3238 Yards, 16 TDs, and 5 INTS)
Player Comparison:Matthew Stafford Detriot Lions

Winner: Deshaun Watson

Leonard Fournette

LSU Runningback Leonard Fournette is a certified beast. If Fournette was allowed to join the 2016 draft, he would have been in consideration for the number one pick. Fournett has bulldozed almost everything in his path. There is talk that him might sit out football this year and join the 2017 NFL Draft. This will ensure that his is healthy for when he joins the NFL. If Fournett does come back to LSU, he is the projected runaway favorite for the Heisman Throphy.
(300 Carries, 1953 Yards, and 22 TDs)
Player Comparison: Adrian Peterson Minnesota Vikings

Dalvin Cook

Florida State Seminoles Dalvin Cook is an elusive all-purpose Runningback. Cook can run from any formation and has the ability to catch the ball from the backfield. In my opinion is better than Ezekial Eliott who went 4th overall in the 2016 draft. Cook should carry the Seminoles to a bowl game regardless of who is throwing the ball for them this year. Look for Cook to be a top ten pick in the 2017 NFL Draft.
